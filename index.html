Executive Summary

I am a data analyst and machine learning engineer specialized in developing solutions for public safety applications, with particular expertise in geospatial analysis, real-time ML inference, and ETL pipeline architecture. My background spans collision data analysis, statistical modeling, and full-stack web development—enabling me to bridge the gap between raw operational datasets and polished, interactive dashboards that inform police resource allocation and urban planning decisions. I am proficient in Python, PostgreSQL, Power BI, and ArcGIS, and I approach each project with an emphasis on rigor, reproducibility, and practical business impact. Whether building YOLO-based computer vision systems or designing predictive traffic forecasting models, I focus on creating solutions that are technically sophisticated, ethically considerate and operationally useful.
